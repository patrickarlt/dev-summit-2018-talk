## As a "designer" you do some/all of this…
## Design is how things function.

What defines "good" design?

  • Meets its criteria
  • Achieves its objectives
  • Functions well
  • Users are happy

Being a good designer is more about asking the right questions then anything else

* What problem are you trying to solve? * Who was it designed for? * What was it supposed to do? * Were users able to accomplish their task? * Were the users happy? * What was the success criteria?
## Learn how to ask the right questions.
## If you end up with a bad design you either: * Didn't ask the right questions. * Failed to address your questions.
