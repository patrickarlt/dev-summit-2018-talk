## As a "designer" you do some/all of this…
## Design is how things work Design is an practial application.

What defines "good" design?

  • Meets its criteria
  • Achieves its objectives
  • Functions well
  • Users are happy
# Being a designer isn't about making something look pretty
# Is is about asking questions * What problem are you trying to solve? * What are the success criteria? * Who are your users? * What was it supposed to do? * Are users able to accomplish their task? * Were the users happy?
## Learn how to ask the right questions.
## If you end up with a bad design you either: * Didn't ask the right questions. * Failed to answer your questions.
